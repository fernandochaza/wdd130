Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to show Caleta Olivia District's information, such as:

  • Leadership composition and branches presidencies
  • District activities and events calendar
  • District History

Audience

This website is for district members and leaders who want to see district’s calendar, and for people wanting to know more about our district.

Content

Home page

This page will contain a banner with a Jesus Christ image as a background or another related image.

The BANNER TEXT will be this:
"Welcome to Caleta Olivia’s District. The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints".

Below the BANNER this text will be placed:
Our district is located in the Argentinian Patagonia. The head is in Caleta Olivia’s Branch, an 80.000 population city in the north of Santa Cruz province.

The district extends to the south and west covering Santa Cruz's north with 4 branches:

  • Caleta Olivia
  • Pico Truncado
  • Las Heras
  • Puerto Deseado

Next there will be a grid with 2 Images and buttons containing links and texts to the child pages.

  • District History: How the church was established in Santa Cruz.
  • District Leadership: People serving in our district.
